 Hello everyone and welcome to today's project discussion. My name is Pankaj and today I want to talk about a project that I am currently building called Summly. Summly is an AI-powered meeting intelligence platform designed to help users transform meeting recordings into structured and searchable knowledge. The main problem that somebody tries to solve is that people attend many meetings every week but most of the valuable information discussed during those meetings is eventually forgotten. Team members often struggle to remember decisions, action, items, deadlines and important discussions. As a result, productivity decreases and information gets lost over time. The goal of Summly is to automatically process meeting recordings and generate useful outputs such as transcripts, summaries, key decisions, action items and searchable organization knowledge. Instead of manual writing, meetings, notes, users can upload or recording and let the system do the heavy lifting. From a learning perspective, this project is helping me understand artificial intelligence neural natural language processing, speech recognition, vector database, retrieval augmented generation and full stack application development. Rather than studying these concepts separately, I am learning them by building a real world product. Thank you for listening to this recording.
